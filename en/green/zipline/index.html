WHAT TO WEAR

We recommend you wear clothes that you don’t mind getting dirty, as the equipemt can be wet with dew and drizzle. Please note Hokkaido has a mild summer climate and temperatures can change dramatically, we recommend you bring a light windproof jacket (preferably waterproof).

FOOTWEAR

Please wear a comfortable pair of cycling or running shoes.

WHAT TO BRING

A CHANGE OF CLOTHES・TOWELS

Please ensure you bring the above as you may get wet.

OTHER VALUABLES

Please alway store them in the lockers provided.

GUESTS UNDER THE INFLUENCE OF ALCOHOL MAY NOT PARTICIPATE IN ACTIVITIES
