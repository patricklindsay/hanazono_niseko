MOUNTAIN BIKING

Hokkaido’s most comprehensive purpose built bike park, designed by competition riders for riders of all levels. Start your day with a biking adventure from the 308 Day Centre, where you have the convenience of rental bikes, café, lockers, showers, toilets and mountain bike accessories. The Park itself contains:

  • Skills development
  • Jump park
  • Pump track
  • X country trails with plenty of exciting features

The park is a perfect place to learn the basics of Mountain Biking or hone your competitive skills to the highest level.


Dates of Operation June 16 - September 25, 2016
