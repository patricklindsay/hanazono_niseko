WHAT TO EXPECT

WHAT TO WEAR

We recommend you wear clothing that you don’t mind getting dirty. Please note Hokkaido has a mild summer climate and temperatures can change dramatically, we recommend you bring a light windproof jacket (preferably waterproof).

SUNGLASSES

We recomend you wear a pair of sunglasses to protect your eyes from bugs and stones.

FOOTWEAR

Please wear a comfortable pair of cycling or running shoes.

WHAT TO BRING

UNDERWEAR・A CHANGE OF CLOTHES・TOWELS

Please ensure you bring the above as you may get wet.

OTHER VALUABLES

Please alway store them in the lockers provided.

GUESTS UNDER THE INFLUENCE OF ALCOHOL MAY NOT PARTICIPATE IN ACTIVITIES
