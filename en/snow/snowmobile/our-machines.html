SAFETY

First timers can be assured our expert guides will explain all equipment, safety features and show you the correct techniques so you can quickly adapt to enjoy the tour. We introduce you to your snowmobile and do some easy practice loops to make sure you're handling your machine comfortably. We set off for the mountain, making plenty of stops to let you catch your breath and take pictures. For safety purposes, we only allow one rider per snowmobile and do not offer tandem rides.

WHAT TO BRING

We recommend you bring the following items for your comfort:

  • Ski/snowboard clothes
  • Goggles
  • Gloves
  • Neck Warmer
  • Beanie
  • Snow Boots

If necessary we can provide Goggles and Helmets.

  • Helmets will be issued and required for all participants
  • Ski helmets are not permitted
  • Ski Boots are not recommended

MEETING POINT

Tours depart from HANAZONO 308. Please meet at the main activities desk in HANAZONO 308, where your guide will fit you with a helmet and necessary equipment for your tour. Catch the HANAZONO Shuttle bus direct to HANAZONO 308 direct from the main village of Hirafu, see the shuttle bus routes below.

HANAZONO SHUTTLE BUS

We've made it hassle free to start your day during winter, with 6 free shuttle buses running guests to HANAZONO 308. We have the entire Hirafu village and surrounded area covered with a bus every 20 minutes; operating through the upper and lower village to transport guests from their door direct to HANAZONO. No one will have to wait or walk far to start their day, this service is ideal for the family or the keen POW enthusiast with the first bus arriving at HANAZONO at 8.20am in time for first lift at 8.30am.

The HANAZONO Shuttle operates daily from 7:50am to 5:10pm
