NISEKO WEISS
POWDER CATS

The Entire mountain.
All day long. Maximum 12 guests.

Ever dreamt of having an entire mountain to yourself? Never having to worry about the lift queue or other skiers/snowboarders, being able to reach the summit without having to lift a finger...
Turn your dream into reality with NISEKO WEISS POWDER CATS.
A full day tour of up to 12 guests that includes lunch, safety gear and pick-up/drop-off to Hirafu accommodations.

GROUP TOUR
PRIVATE TOUR

RESORT OPERATED

NISEKO WEISS POWDER CATS is operated by Niseko HANAZONO Resort.
Have all the experience and expertise of the ski resort behind your cat ski tour.

FIRST TRACKS

The venue of the NISEKO WEISS POWDER CATS is Mt. Weisshorn, once a ski resort but currently not used by anyone but us. This means the entire mountain is available for private use for parties up to 12 people.
One big mountain, only 12 people...
That makes it difficult NOT to have first tracks.

OUR CAT

Some guests may not be familiar with the word “CAT”.
Perhaps you have seen those large vehicles grooming the ski fields, that is one type of CAT.
The machine we use has a specially-made cabin that can fit up to 12 guests, keeping them warm during the ride up. We also have snacks and water prepared to satisfy your hunger and quench your thirst.

BOOK EARLY

Only one tour a day.
Up to 12 guests maximum per tour. A very limited number of guests can take part in the NISEKO WEISS POWDER CATS.
We strongly recommend guests to secure their seat in advance.

Dates of Operation December 20, 2016 – Late March, 2017
