WHAT LEVEL IS MY CHILD AT NISS?

Skiing and riding is about having fun – and being safe while doing it. This guide will help you understand your child's skiing and snowboarding ability and what level to sign them up for.

LEVEL 1
SKIERS SNOWBOARDERS
I've never skied/cannot stop - let's go! I've never snowboarded/cannot stop - let's go!
LEVEL 2
SKIERS SNOWBOARDERS
I can snowplough glide and am learning to snowplough turn. I can control direction/speed by falling leaf and am learning to link heel and toe side turns.
LEVEL 3
SKIERS SNOWBOARDERS
I can snowplough turn and am learning to ski parallel. I can make linked turns on all green terrain and am learning to change turn shape and rhythm.
LEVEL 4
SKIERS SNOWBOARDERS
I can ski on all green, most reds and am learning parallel, short turns, easy bumps and powder. I can make refined turns on all red terrain and am learning carving turns, riding switch.
LEVEL 5
SKIERS SNOWBOARDERS
I can ski parallel all over the mountain and am learning to make dynamic turns, fall line bumps and powder. I can snowboard all over the mountain and strengthening off-piste, powder and terrain park skills.

WHAT DOES MY CHILD NEED FOR A LESSON?

LIFT PASS: We recommend you provide your child with a lift ticket regardless of their level, children under the age of 7 can receive a free ticket when accompanied by a parent (A 1,000yen deposit is required).

EQUIPMENT: To make the experience easy and fun, please ensure your child is familiar and comfortable with their ski/snowboard equipment. Rentals are available from Niseko Sports outlets in Hirafu and HANAZONO.

CLOTHING: Dress your child in base, mid and outer layers to prepare for changing weather conditions. A waterproof jacket and pants, gloves or mittens, goggles and helmet are a must. Having your child practice with all the unfamiliar ski wear ahead of time will help them to have a more enjoyable experience.
