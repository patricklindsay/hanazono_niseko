SHUTTLE BUS SERVICE

NISS EXPRESS SHUTTLE BUS

We've made it hassle free to start your day's group lessons with our NISS Express Shuttle Service from NISS HIRAFU direct to HANAZONO. Simply arrive for registration before 9:15am and your instructor will be waiting to assist you with the short 15 minute trip to HANAZONO. Your instructor will provide a short introduction on the way to prepare you for your day's lesson. We also provide continuous min-shuttles throughout the day.

HANAZONO SHUTTLE BUS

We also have 6 free HANAZONO Shuttle buses running all through the day. We have the entire Hirafu village and surrounded area covered with a bus every 20 minutes; operating through the upper and lower village to transport guests from their door direct to HANAZONO. The HANAZONO Shuttle operates daily from 7:50am to 5:10pm.
