Back Country Guides

World’s best powder.
Share your passion and experience with guests from all over the world.

HANAZONO POWDER GUIDES (HPG) is a team of back country guides working as a part of the Niseko HANAZONO Ski Resort. HPG introduces the Niseko powder to those guests from all parts of the world.
Starting new from the 2015-16 season is the snow cat tour at the Niseko Weiss Mountain.
We are now seeking for passionate members who would share the perfect powder moment with our guests.

We would like to welcome those;

  • - Skiers/Snowboarders/Telemarkers with good back country experience.
  • - Who has all necessary back country equipment.
  • - Who understands the “Niseko Rules” well and has experience riding the back country areas accessible through the Niseko gates.
  • - Who is passionate and is open-minded to exploring new & different culture.
  • - Who has qualiﬁcation in avalanche control and/or is at the process of achieving one.
  • - Who has any other qualifications related to guiding.
  • - Who has official license to drive in Japan (in snow.)

Please email your CV and references to;
employment@harmonyresorts.com

Any and all inquires are welcome at the above email address.
