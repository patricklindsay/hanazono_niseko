Development

Hanazono–World Class–All Season–Luxury Resort

Pacific Century Premium Developments is proud to present HANAZONO, Niseko, the world–class, all–season luxury resort located in the southwest corner of Japan's northernmost island of Hokkaido.

Hanazono Resort lies 1000 metres below the spectacular peak of Mount Niseko Annupuri, offering you one vertical kilometer of stunning powder snow in the winter, an unprecedented variety of activities during the rest of the year including golfing, watersports, eco–trekking as well as year–round enjoyment of the colourful tapestry of lush landscape, the unique history and culture and the incredible array of local produce and international cuisine. To end your day, rejuvenate your senses with a trip to the onsen.

The Pacific Century Group focuses on high–end hospitality and property projects in Asia. After the success of Pacific Century Place in Beijing and Tokyo as well as Bel–Air in Hong Kong, we are preparing for the sales launch of our Hanazono resort in Niseko Japan and our Phang–Nga resort, north of Phuket in Thailand. If you are interested in how you can enrich your holiday experience in these two pristine resort destinations in Asia, please contact our resort ambassadors in Hong Kong at (852)28788878 or in Japan at (81)136216655
