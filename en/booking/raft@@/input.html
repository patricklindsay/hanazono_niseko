Online Booking Form

You will receive a 10% discount when you book rafting tours using this online booking form.
The discount does not apply to bookings made via phone or email.
Please pay in cash on the day of your tour.

Please fill out the inquiry form below and click the “Confirm details” button.
Please note on receipt of the booking inquiry, our staff will contact you and provide a confirmation of availability. Until such time the booking remains tentative.
For bookings on the day or one day before the tour, please call (0136) 21 3333
